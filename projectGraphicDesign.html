Graphic Design Explorations

During my first semester of college, I took an intro to graphic design class. From this experience, I learned how to use popular Adobe software programs such as Photoshop and Illustrator. Shown here are three of my projects from that class. The first is a drawing of a living room made in Adobe Illustrator. The second is an outdoor scene at an amusement park made using Photoshop. The third is an informational poster about a typography designer that I created with Adobe InDesign.
