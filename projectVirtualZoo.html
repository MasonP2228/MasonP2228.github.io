Virtual Zoo

Building on my previous VR project, I was tasked with creating a more immersive VR environment using Unreal Engine. I decided to make a virtual visit to the zoo, with numerous animal exhibits to look at and pathways to go down.
From this project, I learned how to allow for player movement in a virtual reality setup. I also learned about lighting techniques and proximity audio, which I used to add sound effects to my animal exhibits.
The final virtual reality level was playable on a university-provided headset. All assets were sourced online.
