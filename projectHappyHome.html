Happy Home

Happy Home is a video game with a focus on urban planning principles. In the game, players create their own digital towns by placing a variety of buildings and plants on a map. Through the use of a "happiness score," players receive feedback for their town designs. In this way, Happy Home encourages its players to think about mindful urban planning practices.
Happy Home was created using Unity Engine. This was my first project in Unity, so I learned a lot! I also got to practice my programming skills with the C# language.
