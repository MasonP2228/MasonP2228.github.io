Architecture Explorations

In 2024, I was a part of an architecture studio class. I was tasked with designing a pavilion for a given site in Burbank. Throughout the class, I learned more about the architectural profession and the process of designing a structure. I gained experience making simple 3D renderings, communicating design information, and building architectural models. I also programmed a microcontroller to use a servo motor as a very rough proof-of-concept for my final design.
