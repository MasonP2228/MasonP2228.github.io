Woodbury Wind

In the fall of 2025, I took a class that focused on using computer software to create various simulations of the built environment. My assigned topic for the class's semester-long project was wind.
Over the course of the semester, I produced a series of posters and a physical model showing wind in relation to the Business Building on my university's campus. My first poster focuses on initial data collection. The second shows how I created a computer simulation in Grasshopper to visualize wind. The third and fourth posters highlight the wind at different times of year and the physical model that I produced, respectively.
