hm2

In my final semester of my undergraduate degree, I was tasked with creating a fictitious business with a partner. My partner and I came up with hm2, a web design and branding company. We designed stationery, business cards, a brochure, a Figma mock-up of a website, and other promotional materials. Throughout the course of the semester, we also learned about topics such as business practices, budgets, and company structures.
