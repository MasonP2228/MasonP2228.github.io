Crystal Cocoon Chair

Made out of only cardboard and hot glue, this geometrically-designed chair is strong enough to support a person's full weight. The high back and sides convey the feeling of being inside a comfortable cocoon, while the geometric form looks crystalline. I made this chair with a small group, and I personally contributed by using Grasshopper to generate some of the form, by formatting the final Rhino file for output to a CAD machine, and by assembling the chair.
