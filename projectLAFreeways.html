Analyzing LA's Freeways

In the fall of 2024, I learned about Geographic Information Systems. Over the course of the semester, I gained experience using GIS software from Esri, including ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Pro, StoryMaps, QuickCapture, and Survey123. The semester culminated in a final project in which I analyzed LA's freeways from a variety of perspectives. I made a map that shows the most congested parts of the freeways, and another that highlights the areas and demographics that are most affected by air pollution. In addition to these, I also created a map that identifies parks and schools that are in close proximity to freeways, and are thereby the most likely to be affected by smog. I compiled all three of these maps together into an interactive web page, which provided context and explanations to the project. I am hoping to share the web page here soon (pending sharing permissions).
