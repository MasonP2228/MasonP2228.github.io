Grasshopper Explorations

In the Spring of 2023, I was first introduced to the Grasshopper visual programming tool. Grasshopper is a sub-program of the popular 3D modeling software Rhino. That spring, I learned the basics of Grasshopper, and I also focused on creating point attractor systems. The pictures to the right show some abstract geometries that were manipulated using these techniques.
