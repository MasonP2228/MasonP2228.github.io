Welcome to CSGeo, the browser-based 3D‑modeling application!

The controls for CSGeo are arranged in 3 sections. The top section contains controls for adding new shapes to your scene. The right section contains controls for selecting, moving, and deleting shapes. The left section contains controls for CSG operations. CSG operations combine 2 shapes to make a new one! Select your second shape with the 2nd button, make sure your two shapes are overlapping, and try it out!

There are also 3 ways to control CSGeo. Use mouse controls by clicking on buttons. Use keyboard controls by pressing the keys associated with each button. Or, use hand controls through your webcamera after clicking the hand icon button.

Happy modeling!

To use hand controls, move your hand in view of your web camera. A hand icon will appear, and it will follow the motion of your index finger.

To click on things, touch the tip of your thumb with your index finger. The icon will change when a click has been registered.

Troubleshooting

If the hand icon does not appear, check to make sure that your finger is in view of the web camera.

If the hand icon is not behaving as expected, please make sure that only one hand is in view of the web camera.

If you are having difficulty clicking, you may need to move your hand closer or further away from the web camera.
