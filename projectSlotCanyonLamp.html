Slot Canyon Lamp

The slot canyons of the American Southwest provided the inspiration for this parametrically-designed lamp. Grasshopper (a visual programming software) was used almost exclusively to craft the design and to generate a fabrication-ready file for Woodbury University's CNC machine. From this project, I gained experience with the Grasshopper software, and I also became more familiar with the production of physical objects.
