Special Packaging Internship

During the summer of 2025, I was fortunate to intern at Spark Brighter Thinking, a packaging and design company. In my internship role, I worked on the structural designs for special packages. From this experience, I learned how to use the ArtiosCAD software program, operate a Kongsberg CAD Table, and assemble white samples. I also got to tour two different manufacturing facilities during the course of my internship.
The pictures shown here are packages that I made. Several of them were created from highly-adjustable parametric design templates that I developed in ArtiosCAD.
